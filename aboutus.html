HGM's History

Harry's Gaming Mouse, founded in 2010 by Harry Lourds de Leon, quickly gained recognition for its innovative peripherals. Over the years, the company introduced features like customizable RGB lighting and ergonomic designs, earning industry awards and global acclaim. Expanding its product line and forming key partnerships, Harry's Gaming Mouse became a trusted name in gaming peripherals. Today, it remains committed to excellence, innovation, and empowering gamers worldwide with top-quality products and cutting-edge technology.
